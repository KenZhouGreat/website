We are Hey Inc. And we're building something that we think you'll like.

We're a passionate team of generalists filling a hole in the universe by building software that enriches the human experience. Everyone on our team codes and contributes on every level. We've all built web scale products and scaled them to millions of daily active users with zero marketing spent.

Alex Le, VP of Product

@alexcharlie alexcharlie alexle.org

Alex is one of the founders of Hey, Inc. and has participated in the development of Heyday in a variety of ways from contributing code to pushing pixels. Before that Alex was the co-creator and head of product management for CityVille and was the CTO and co-founder of Serious Business (acquired by Zynga).

Bryn Kass, Operations Generalist

@bryn_kass blogspot.com

Before joining Hey Inc., Bryn was a teacher at The Institute of Reading Development, where she taught reading skills to children and adults. Bryn was class president for four years straight at Tufts University and a competitive cross country and marathon runner.

Jameson Lee, Systems Generalist

@jamesonjlee jamesonjlee github.io

Siqi Chen, CEO

Timothy Kautz, Product Generalist
